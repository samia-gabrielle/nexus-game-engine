**The 4 scenes of Act 1 (2150):**

   - You wake in a glass tower, no memories
   - Meet ECHO (the AI)
   - Get first glimpse of the world
   - **Choice:** Trust the AI or be suspicious?

2. **SCENE 2: DESCENT**
   - Explore the megacity
   - Meet COMPANION (first NPC)
   - Learn about your mysterious past
   - **Choice:** Ask directly or investigate secretly?

3. **SCENE 3: DISCOVERY**
   - Find clues about who you were
   - Major reveal: You've been here before
   - **Choice:** Accept the mystery or demand answers?

4. **SCENE 4: THE MACHINE**
   - Find the time travel device
   - Learn you can access other universes
   - **Choice:** Jump immediately or wait for more info?

## 💭 Key Questions to Answer (For Yourself)

Before writing, think about:

1. **What is NEXUS really about?**
   - My thematic twist is: The search for meaning in a world where you can access infinite versions of yourself
   - What's YOUR interpretation?

2. **Who is the Companion?**
   - In my design, they're your guide and questioner
   - Who do you imagine them as? Age? Background? Personality?

3. **What's the tone?**
   - Should it be:
     - Melancholic and contemplative?
     - Tense and mysterious?
     - Philosophical and questioning?
   - Mix of all three?

4. **The Big Mystery:**
   - What's the ONE thing the player desperately wants to know?
   - (My answer: "Who am I, really?" / What's yours?)
