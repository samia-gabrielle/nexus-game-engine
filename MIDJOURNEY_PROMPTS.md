## **Tips for Best Results:**

1. **Use `--niji 6`** for manga/graphic novel style
2. **Use `--ar 16:9`** for cinematic aspect ratio
3. **Use `--quality 2`** for more detail (costs more credits)
4. **Describe mood FIRST** ("melancholic noir"), then technical details
5. **Reference artists:** Add `in style of Moebius` or `ink drawing` if needed
6. **Color keywords matter:** Cyan, noir, amber, bioluminescent, etc.

---

## **Generation Strategy:**

**Day 1: Generate all 18 prompts (5 credits each = ~90 credits)**
- Time: ~3 hours
- Cost: ~$20-30 (Midjourney monthly plan)
- Generate 4 variations each

**Day 2: Curate best 5-7**
- Select winners based on:
  - Mood/tone match
  - Visual clarity
  - Uniqueness
  - Consistency with NEXUS aesthetic

**Day 3-7: Commission artista**
- Send 5-7 selected images to artist
- Brief: "Refine in graphic novel style, keep mood/composition"
- Timeline: 2-3 weeks (parallel with other work)
