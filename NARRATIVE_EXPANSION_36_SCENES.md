## 🎨 VISUAL TONE GUIDE

**DO:**
- ✅ Graphic novel style (bold ink linework, high contrast)
- ✅ Cinematic noir (dramatic lighting, psychological weight)
- ✅ Timeline-specific palettes:
  - 1920: Sepia, burnt sienna, warm golds, candlelight
  - 1970: Ochre, warm greens, institutional grays, fabric textures
  - 2050: Clinical white, cold cyan, institutional blues, neon accents
  - 2150: Deep blacks, neon cyan/magenta, acid rain highlights
  - Mystical: Purples, whites, glowing auras, dreamlike distortion

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Photorealism (too modern/disconnected)
- ❌ Bright colors (contradicts noir tone)
- ❌ Soft-focus/fantasy (doesn't match hard sci-fi)
- ❌ Inconsistent character designs (Mara/Reva/Thomas must be recognizable across scenes)
