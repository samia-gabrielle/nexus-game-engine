                           GAME BASICS
================================================================================

The Story:
  You wake in NEXUS, an underground city in 2150. The dimensional barriers
  protecting civilization are failing. You discover you're a "Bridge"—a
  consciousness existing across multiple dimensions.

  Your choices will determine the fate of billions across parallel realities.

How to Play:
  • Read the scene description
  • Look at the scene image
  • Click a button to make a choice
  • Watch the story unfold based on your decisions

What to Track:
  • Relationships panel (left sidebar) - shows how characters feel about you
  • Caderno - collects story entries and lore
  • Ecos - marks important narrative moments
  • Playtime - shows how long you've been playing

Controls:
  • Mouse: Click buttons to advance
  • Touch: Tap buttons on mobile/tablet
  • Audio: Toggle music and sound effects in settings

================================================================================
                            SAVE SYSTEM
================================================================================

Auto-Save:
  ✅ Progress saved automatically after each scene
  ✅ Saves your position, relationships, entries, ecos
  ✅ Survives browser restart (stored in localStorage)

Manual Save:
  • Click "Salvar Jogo" in settings to create explicit save
  • Useful before making difficult choice
  • Can have one active save at a time

Load Game:
  • Click "Carregar Jogo" to resume from last save
  • Picks up exactly where you left off
  • All progress preserved

Reset Game:
  • Click "Reiniciar Jogo" to start completely fresh
  • WARNING: Deletes all progress
  • Useful if you want to try different choices

Pro Tip:
  Try different choices, use manual save before key decisions,
  then load to explore alternate paths.
