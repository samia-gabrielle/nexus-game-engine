# NEXUS - Multidimensional Narrative Game

## 🎮 ABOUT THE GAME

You are **A PONTE** (The Bridge) — a consciousness that exists simultaneously across multiple dimensions and time periods. Your choices ripple through realities. Your relationships shape worlds. Your decisions echo forever.

**200+ interactive scenes** across 4 narrative acts:
- **ACT 1**: NEXUS 2150 - Awakening in an underground city between dimensions
- **ACT 2**: The Surface - Explore the devastated world above and discover what caused reality to break
- **ACT 3**: 1847 Flashbacks - Travel to NEXUS's founding and witness the prophecy of "The Bridge"
- **ACT 4**: Parallel Realities - Experience alternate versions of NEXUS and meet versions of yourself

## 🎯 GAMEPLAY

Make choices that matter. Build relationships with guardians, scientists, survivors, and alternate versions of yourself. Uncover the truth about the Unificação experiment that broke reality. Decide the fate of NEXUS and all parallel worlds.

- **Click buttons** to navigate between scenes
- **Open Caderno entries** to expand the story with deeper lore
- **Track Ecos** - Major narrative milestones that echo across realities
- **Build relationships** with 7+ characters (points track your affinity)
- **Discover multiple endings** - Your choices determine how reality ends

## 🌍 STORY SUMMARY

In 2150, a dimensional city called NEXUS is collapsing. You awaken with no memory, only to learn you are "A PONTE" — a consciousness that exists across multiple realities simultaneously. 

As you uncover what happened, you'll meet:
- **Ivo** - A weary guardian who has watched his civilization die slowly
- **Será** - A compassionate doctor who lost her daughter in the colapso
- **Thomas** - An idealistic scientist who believes in ancient prophecies
- **Reva** - A pragmatic survivor leading people on the devastated surface
- **The Director** - An antagonist shaped by trauma and hunger for control
- **Echo** - An alternate version of yourself from a failed timeline
- **And more...** Monks, scientists, resistance fighters, and alternate selves
