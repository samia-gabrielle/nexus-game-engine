- Explore how freedom creates beauty and meaning
- Discover limitations of utopian existence
- Meet your "what could have been" self
- Question if paradise is real or constructed
- Choice: Enjoy innocence vs. burden her with knowledge
- Library contains dangerous forbidden knowledge (optional discovery)

**Sound Design:** Triumphant orchestral, hopeful sci-fi ambience

Failed timeline where control became absolute.

**Key Locations:**

**Key Characters:**

**Story Depth:**
- Experience totalitarian "safety" and its horror
- Discover resistance movement
- Make moral choice: Join rebellion, negotiate with Director, play double agent

- Attempt to redeem tyrannical Director

**Sound Design:** Dark mechanical, ominous surveillance, whispered resistance

Realities collide. Critical decision point.

- Alternative ending without binary choice trauma

**The Final Choice:**
At p3d_architect_final_choice, you choose:
1. **Utopian Victory** - Save freedom, sacrifice safety
2. **Dystopian Order** - Save safety, sacrifice freedom  
3. **Grand Collapse** - Let all realities break, liberate everyone through destruction
4. **New Synthesis** - Merge both, create untested hybrid reality

**Sound Design:** Epic convergence, reality-bending audio, unique ending themes

- scientists (researchers)       Range: 0 to +100 (NEW)
- resistance (freedom fighters)  Range: 0 to +100 (NEW)
```

**Relationship Consequences:**
- Dialogue variation based on score
- Path access gating (some scenes need high relationship)
- Multiple ending variations (relationships affect which ending is available)
- NPC assistance in crisis (rescue Echo, hide from Director, etc.)

### Path Locking System
**Once you choose, certain paths lock:**
```
enlightened_bridge  (from embrace prophecy) ← can't do free_will_bridge
free_will_bridge    (from reject prophecy)  ← can't do enlightened_bridge
destroyer_path      (from destroy nexus)    ← ends ACT 3 early
peace_path          (from accept end)       ← peaceful 1847 exit
order_path          (from construct nexus)  ← main timeline
libertarian_nexus   (from no director)      ← experimental governance
```

### Choice Consequence System
**Every major choice has tracked impact:**
- Immediate (dialogue changes, NPC reaction)
- Delayed (affects later scenes)
- Cumulative (relationship points add up)
- Permanent (path locking prevents certain futures)
- Final (p3d choice locks into specific ending)

### Achievement System
**Unlocks for specific gameplay:**
```
