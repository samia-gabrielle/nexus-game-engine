# NEXUS - Installation & Setup Guide

## What is NEXUS?

NEXUS is a complete narrative game experience spanning 200+ interactive scenes across multiple dimensions and realities. The game explores themes of identity, choice, sacrifice, and redemption through a branching narrative that adapts to your decisions.

## System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (modern versions, 2020+)
- **OS**: Windows, macOS, Linux (any OS with a modern web browser)
- **Internet**: Not required (game works completely offline)
- **Storage**: ~5MB for single HTML file
- **RAM**: 256MB minimum

## Installation

### Option 1: Local File (Recommended)

1. **Download the Game**
   - Download `nexus-complete.html` from the game folder
   - Save it to your computer (e.g., Desktop, Documents folder)

2. **Play the Game**
   - Double-click `nexus-complete.html`
   - Game opens automatically in your default browser
   - No installation needed

3. **Alternative: Choose Browser**
   - Right-click `nexus-complete.html`
   - Select "Open with..." → Choose your preferred browser
   - Click to play

### Option 2: Web Server (Optional)

If you want to host NEXUS online:

1. **Upload File**
   - Upload `nexus-complete.html` to your web hosting
   - Share the URL with friends

2. **itch.io Publishing** (for distribution)
   - Create an account on itch.io
   - Upload the HTML file
   - Set game as "HTML" type
   - Make it public
   - Share your itch.io game page

## How to Play

### Basic Controls

- **Read the Scene**: Each scene presents a narrative description
- **View Image**: Scene artwork displays automatically (generated or custom)
- **Make Choices**: Click blue buttons at the bottom to advance
- **Track Progress**: Monitor relationships and discoveries in the sidebar

### Game Features

#### 🎮 Core Gameplay
- **200+ Branching Scenes**: Multiple paths through interconnected narratives
- **Dynamic Relationships**: Your choices affect relationships with 7 main characters
- **Caderno (Journal)**: Collect narrative insights and discoveries
- **Ecos (Echoes)**: Milestone moments that shape your journey
- **Play Statistics**: Track time, scenes visited, and choices made

#### 👥 Relationship System
Track your standing with:
- **Ivo** - Wise Guardian of NEXUS
- **Será** - Compassionate Medical Chief
- **Thomas** - Young Hopeful Scientist
- **Reva** - Surface Survivor Leader
- **Director** - Complex Authority Figure
- **Architect** - Mysterious Founder
- **Echo** - Your Alternate Self

Relationships range from -100 to +100:
- **+75 to +100**: Trusted Ally (special scenes unlocked)
- **+25 to +75**: Cooperative
- **-25 to +25**: Neutral
- **-25 to -75**: Distrustful
- **-75 to -100**: Hostile (locked scenes)

#### 📝 Caderno System
Collect narrative entries throughout the game:
- Click any entry to read detailed descriptions
- Entries unlock based on story choices
- Build a comprehensive understanding of NEXUS lore
- Reference guide to character backgrounds and world-building

#### 🔷 Ecos System
Discover profound "echoes" marking story turning points:
- Visual indicators of major narrative milestones
- Represent convergence points across dimensions
- Unlock achievements and epilogue content
- Collectible throughout your playthrough

### Audio & Controls

#### 🎵 Audio Settings
- **Music Toggle**: Enable/disable background atmosphere
- **SFX Toggle**: Enable/disable sound effects (clicks, transitions, echoes)
- **Volume Slider**: Adjust master volume (0-100%)
- Settings persist across sessions

#### 💾 Game Management
- **Auto-Save**: Progress saved automatically after each scene
- **Manual Save**: Click "Salvar Jogo" to create backup save
- **Load Game**: Click "Carregar Jogo" to resume where you left off
- **Reset Game**: Start completely fresh (warning: deletes current progress)

## Multiple Endings

Your choices throughout the game determine which ending you receive:

### 1. **The Sacrifice** (Default)
- Become the permanent Bridge between dimensions
- Save NEXUS and all connected realities
- Transcend individual identity
- Requirement: High Ivo + Será relationship, Propósito scene

### 2. **The Utopian Path**
- Choose to preserve freedom and liberty
- Support Echo and the free citizens
- Accept compromises and challenges
- Requirement: High Echo + Thomas relationship, utopian exploration

### 3. **The Dystopian Path**
- Preserve order and security
- Accept controlled society
- Find peace in structure
- Requirement: High Director relationship, surveillance acceptance

### 4. **The Rejection**
- Refuse to play savior
- Let NEXUS collapse naturally
- Explore the aftermath
- Requirement: Low Ivo relationship, multiple refusals

### 5. **The Balance**
- Attempt synthesis of freedom and order
- Create new hybrid reality
- Accept imperfect solutions
- Requirement: Moderate all relationships, careful choices

## Tips & Strategies

### First Playthrough
- **Don't Optimize**: Make choices that feel right, not "optimal"
- **Explore Modals**: Click on relationships/caderno/ecos to understand lore
- **Take Your Time**: There's no time limit; enjoy the narrative
- **Accept Consequences**: Some choices lock out content permanently

### Replay Value
- **Multiple Paths**: Save your game, then load and try different choices
- **Relationship Routes**: Focus on specific characters to unlock unique scenes
- **Speedrun**: Once familiar, you can reach endings in 30-45 minutes
- **Collection**: Try to collect all 200+ ecos across multiple playthroughs

### Unlocking Hidden Content
- Reach specific relationship thresholds to unlock alternate scenes
- Complete certain combinations of choices for secret epilogues
- Collect all caderno entries for comprehensive understanding
- Discover the "True Ending" (varies based on total relationships)

## Troubleshooting

### Game Won't Load
1. **Verify File**: Make sure `nexus-complete.html` is intact
2. **Browser Issue**: Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox)
3. **Cache Clear**: Clear browser cache and cookies, then reload
4. **JavaScript**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled in browser settings

### Audio Not Working
1. **Check Settings**: Verify music/SFX are enabled in-game
2. **Volume**: Ensure volume slider is above 0%
3. **Browser Audio**: Check browser audio permissions
4. **Device Audio**: Verify device speakers/headphones are working

### Save Game Issues
1. **Check Storage**: Browser storage might be full
2. **Private Mode**: Storage disabled in private/incognito mode
3. **Clear Cache**: Old saves might cause conflicts
4. **Reinstall**: Delete old save with reset button, start fresh

### Performance Issues
1. **Close Background Apps**: Free up system RAM
2. **Browser Extensions**: Try disabling extensions temporarily
3. **Lower Graphics**: Browser graphics acceleration may be off
4. **Restart Browser**: Full browser restart can help
