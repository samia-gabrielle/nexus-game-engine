## 🎯 Story Overview

### Act 1: Awakening (2150)
Wake in NEXUS. Meet guardians. Discover you're a Bridge. Learn about crystal collapse.
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes

### Act 2: Surface Exploration
Journey to dying surface. Meet survivors. Discover uncomfortable truths.
**Duration:** 40-60 minutes

### Act 3: Historical Flashbacks (1847)
Travel to founding of NEXUS. Meet the Architect. Understand dimensional physics.
**Duration:** 30-50 minutes

### Act 4: Parallel Realities
Experience utopian and dystopian versions. Meet your alternate self. Face ultimate questions.
**Duration:** 20-40 minutes
