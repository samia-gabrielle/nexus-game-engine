## 🎨 VISUAL BIBLE (Use across all prompts)

### PROTAGONISTA
- Appearance: East Asian features, gaunt from temporal stress, early 30s but aging poorly
- Hair: dark, slightly long, disheveled
- Eyes: observant, haunted, seeing-through quality
- Clothes: layered noir (dark jacket, worn tech-fabric underneath)
- Wrist symbol: four-line mark, incomplete (3 lines initially, gradually completes)
- Build: lean, worn, carrying invisible weight

### TIMELINE PALETTES
- **1920**: Sepia, burnt sienna, warm gold, aged wood, bronze, rain-silver
- **1970**: Ochre, warm greens, burnt orange, aged paper, institutional browns, textile texture
- **2050**: Clinical white, cold cyan, institutional blue, amber accent lighting, glass/metal
- **2150**: Black, concrete gray, neon cyan, white neon, faded propaganda red
- **Nexus/Temporal**: White-hot, copper/bronze, spectral violet, frozen cyan, void-black

### VISUAL STYLE (FIXED)
**Graphic novel cinematic noir** = bold inked linework + extreme high contrast + textured paper feel + sci-fi hardness + emotional weight

### KEY CHARACTERS (for reference when they appear)
- **Mara**: woman, 40s-50s, sharp features, worn scientist aesthetic, dark hair graying, eyes that have seen impossible things
- **Reva**: woman, 30s, intensity in face, warrior-bearing, organized fierceness, dark eyes
- **Thomas**: androgynous/ambiguous, gaunt face, eyes that see *through* moments, temporal damage visible in bearing
- **Ivo**: non-binary/ambiguous, archivist presence, careful hands, organized precision in chaos
